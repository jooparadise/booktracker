Write / Edit Reflection

Saved Reflections

Writing Coach · 감상평 쓰는 법

감상평 공식: 줄거리 요약 30% + 내 생각 40% + 책 속 근거 20% + 질문/연결 10%.
  1. Summary: 책 전체를 다시 쓰지 말고, 중심 인물·문제·변화만 적기.
  2. Claim: “나는 이 책이 ___에 관한 책이라고 생각한다.”
  3. Evidence: 왜냐하면 ___ 장면에서 ___가 보였기 때문이다.
  4. Connection: 내 경험, 가족, 학교, 사회, 역사와 연결하기.
  5. Question: 작가에게 묻고 싶은 질문 하나 쓰기.
Strong reflection formula: I think + because + evidence from the book + connection + question.

A good response does not only say “I liked it.” It explains what the book made you think, feel, question, or understand.

  • This book is mainly about...
  • The character changes when...
  • I was surprised by...
  • I agree/disagree with the character because...
  • One scene that proves this is...
  • This connects to real life because...
  • After reading, I wonder...
LevelCriteria
4Clear summary, thoughtful opinion, specific evidence, strong connection.
3Good summary and opinion with at least one example.
2Mostly summary, little personal thinking.
1Too short or unclear; needs evidence and explanation.
